Advertising in Events & venues Magazine

The best way to reach your customers

With its concentrated coverage and with every issue produced in full colour glossy paper, what better media for your company to reach our readers. The magazine offers a full consultation and design service including photography, with all images created being of the highest quality to appeal to customers.

With a print run of 14,500 we are distributed directly through letterboxes in Leighton Buzzard and Linslade and are available at pick-up points in Cublington, Eaton Bray, Edlesborough, Heath & Reach, Hockliffe, Northall, Stanbridge, Stewkley, Stoke Hammond, Soulbury, Tilsworth and Wing.

Advertising space is available at full page (B5), half page, quarter page, eighth page (approximately business card size) and 'micro' ads (wording only). Prices start from as little as £45 inc VAT (pre-paid) per issue for a 'micro' ad. So whether you're a sole trader or larger business we have something to suit you.

Ask about the special full page 'advertorial spotlight' designed to give companies the edge amongst the readers.

Prices and series discount structure available on request.
