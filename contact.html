About Events & venues Magazine

Launched in February 2012 by Chris Green, Events & venues Magazine is a showcase for the many clubs, businesses and charities of Leighton Buzzard and surrounding areas.

We go out of our way to celebrate good news stories and the individuals who make them possible. The town has a busy calendar of events from the May Day Celebrations, Carnival, Canal Festival, Bonfire Night Fireworks and beyond. If you're looking for what's on you'll find it in the E&v.

With an initial print run of 10,000 copies delivered to homes we have increased our reach to 14,500 copies, with pick-up points in Leighton, Linslade, Cublington, Eaton Bray, Edlesborough, Heath & Reach, Hockliffe, Northall, Stanbridge, Stewkley, Stoke Hammond, Soulbury, Tilsworth and Wing.

We are proud to be locally owned, run and printed here in Leighton Buzzard!
